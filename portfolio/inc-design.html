Inc Design

Inc Design is a branding & corporate communications design firm located in New York City. The company is led by its founding partners, Martine Chepigin and Bill Ferguson. Services include corporate branding & identity design, CSR reports (corporate sustainability), interactive media & video production, investor communications & annual report design, marketing communications collateral and advertising programs. The website is highly adapted for cross device browser utilizing multiple layouts depending on device. The site also makes tasteful use of JavaScript animations including fading and sliding effects.
