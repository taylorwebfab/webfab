GILEAD - Sales Training APP

Gilead Sciences, Inc. is a research-based bio-pharmaceutical company that discovers, develops and commercializes innovative medicines in areas of unmet medical need.

The Sales Training application was created to help organize, schedule and track new practices as they were rolled out. We created a custom content management system that integrated with two isotope pages and a calender to help easily record the status of tasks and keep all teams on the same page from any device or browser. Users can easily sort and modify tasks with any of the three views.
